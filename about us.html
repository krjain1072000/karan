About Us

A coffee shop located in the University of Surrey that aims to provide affordable, high-quality coffee and snacks to students and faculty. With a focus on community, the shop provides a welcoming atmosphere where people can come together to study, relax, and connect with each other. Tapriya's mission is to provide a comfortable and affordable space for people to enjoy quality coffee and food while fostering a sense of community. The vision is to become the go-to destination for quality coffee and snacks in the University of Surrey area.

Vision

Our vision is to become the go-to destination for everyone in the University of Surrey community who loves quality coffee and delicious snacks. We aim to be known not only for our affordability and sustainability but also for our commitment to creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere that fosters a sense of community. Our ultimate goal is to become an integral part of the University of Surrey experience, where students, faculty, and visitors alike can come together to relax, recharge, and connect over a cup of coffee.

Mission

Our mission is to provide the University of Surrey community with a welcoming and affordable coffee shop experience. We strive to create a cozy and comfortable atmosphere where students, faculty, and visitors can gather to enjoy delicious coffee and a variety of snacks. Our commitment to quality and affordability is reflected in every cup of coffee we serve. We believe that everyone deserves access to great coffee, and we are dedicated to making that a reality. With a focus on sustainability and community, Tapriya is more than just a coffee shop, it's a home base for the University of Surrey.
